About Us

Micro Sense Earthing Solutions

Micro Sense Earthing Solutions is a trusted and leading manufacturer of Chemical Earthing and Lightning Protection Systems based in Maharashtra. With a strong commitment to safety, innovation, and quality, we specialize in the design and production of Lightning Arresters and Earthing Electrodes, proudly marketed under the brand name Micro Sense. All our products are engineered in compliance with the latest Indian (IS) and International (IEC/IEEE) standards, ensuring maximum safety, durability, and performance in even the most demanding environments. At Micro Sense, we are driven by a mission to provide reliable earthing and lightning protection solutions for a wide range of applications including: Residential and commercial buildings Industrial plants and factories Electrical substations
